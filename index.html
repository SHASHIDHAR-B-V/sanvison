About the Artist

Where light
tells stories

With over 15 years behind the lens, I have developed an obsessive eye for the interplay of shadow and light.

From the fog-laced streets of Lisbon to the neon-drenched alleys of Tokyo, my work spans editorial fashion, intimate portraiture, and fine art landscapes.

My images have graced the covers of Vogue Italia and Harper's Bazaar. Every project receives the same devotion to craft.
